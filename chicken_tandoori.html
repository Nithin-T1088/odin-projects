How to prepare CHICKEN TANDOORI


Get ready to experience one of the most beloved and instantly recognizable dishes in Indian cuisine: Tandoori Chicken. Far more than just grilled chicken, this dish is a culinary spectacle, famous worldwide for its vivid red hue, smoky char, and incredibly tender, juicy meat.

Ingredients :

  • Chicken
  • Lemon Juice
  • salt
  • Curd
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Black Pepper
  • Ground Cumin
  • Ground Coriander
  • Oil
  • Kasthuri Methi

Procedure :

  1. Use a sharp knife to make 3-4 deep slits on each piece of chicken (thighs, drumsticks, etc.).
  2. Rub the chicken thoroughly with lemon juice and salt. Let it sit for 10-15 minutes. This step helps the spices penetrate better.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the Greek yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, all the ground spices (Kashmiri chili, garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric), and a dash of oil.
  4. Add the prepped chicken to the bowl. Use your hands to massage the marinade deep into the slits and crevices.
  5. over the bowl and refrigerate for a minimum of 4 hours, or ideally, overnight (8-12 hours), for maximum flavor and tenderness.
  6. Preheat your oven to a high temperature (around 400 deg F / 200 deg C), or preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  7. Place the chicken on a rack over a baking tray (or directly on the grill grates). Bake or grill until the chicken is cooked through and the edges are nicely charred.
  8. In the last 5 minutes of cooking, brush the chicken with a little melted butter or ghee.
  9. Remove from heat, let it rest briefly, and serve immediately with fresh lemon wedges and mint raita.
